projects

Course-assistant application project or "Teamspot" is a project to help people find teammates in team-based class, where people in that class may have difficulties to identify the proper teammates who have skills people need. Please view the website to see the demo.

This project was developed during the peak of COVID-19. Many pepole were still refusing to put masks on their faces. While many cs people worked on mask detection, most their work were based on high-resolution videos. To fill the gap, this project applied innovative models to detect mask-wearing through low-resolution videos. The code could be checked.

The "stay home" policy altered social interaction since the pandemic. People chose online interaction either voluntarily or involuntarily. VR applications were more accepted under this situation. This project aimed to explore the newly growing online community. Analyzing people's behaviors on the community, how they interact with each other, compare and contrast the online community with the traditional community, and investigate the interaction via VR device. . The study report is available to be download.

YouTube/Google published its recommendation system. It made me wonder the recommendation systems in other online video stream platforms. I studied a similar company called BiliBili. Although BiliBili doesn't publish its recommendation system, many video makers and people like me analyzed BiliBili's recommendation system. In short, BiliBili spreads popular videos based on viewers' feedbacks and BiliBili's system doesn't focus on individual users. I also postulated that the recommendation system was relevant to business models. Then I provide my analysis and reasons. . The study report is available to be download.

For people who lack motivations and strength to achieve their exercise goal or don't know how to setup the exercise goal, this project was designed to help thme. I explored the interaction methods which could promote people's exerice and get them involved more. The project also designed social functionality and reward system. I refined designing ideas from 100 to 4. . The work is available to view.
